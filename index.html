Think you know your Prince from your Queen? Your Beatles from your Stones? Take the ultimate music challenge with a Bytown Quiz tailored to your audience, large or small.

Tim McGurrin has been building and hosting music quizzes for more than 10 years, raising money for a wide variety of charitable organizations, including the United Way, Canada Post Community Foundation, NAJDA Syrian Refugee Sponsorship group and The Caring and Sharing Network.

If you’re looking for fun group event for your workplace or private function, why not try a Bytown Quiz?

The standard quiz is a selection of song snippets from a variety of genres and eras, built around a theme (animals, colours, proper names, etc.) and lasting between 60 and 90 minutes. The audience is divided into small teams of 4 or 5 players and each team works together to try and guess the song titles, artists or other information related to the song.

I supply all of the necessary equipment (speakers and microphones) and act as the host/MC throughout the evening, so you can enjoy the party and even play along if you like!
